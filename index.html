About Gold Gym

Your local gym, done right.

Gold Gym is a neighbourhood fitness centre in the heart of Proddatur, located above Creamstone in YMR Colony. We keep things simple: a well-maintained space, the essentials you actually use, and a crowd that shows up to train — not to pose.

Whether you're starting your first workout or getting back after a break, the doors open early so you can train before the day gets busy.

  • Early-morning opening (from 5:30 am)
  • Centrally located in YMR Colony, Proddatur
  • Rated 5.0 ★ by the local community
  • Friendly for beginners and regulars alike
